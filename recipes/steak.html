Steak

Description

This recipe is as old as time, and has been a staple of human cuisines throughout the many vicissitudes of life.

Ingredients

  • Grass-Fed Ribeye Steak: 16 ounces
  • Grass-Fed Butter or Extra Virgin Italian Olive Oil
  • Cast Iron or Stainless Steel Pan
  • Oven and Stove-top
  • Time: Approximately 25 minutes

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 250 degrees F.
  2. Pre-heat the pan to medium for 3 minutes.
  3. Once the pan is hot add butter/olive oil.
  4. Add steak to pan, cook for 3 minutes, flip to other side, cook for another 3 minutes (6 minutes total cook-time).
  5. Remove pan from stove-top and put into the oven for 10 minutes.
  6. Remove pan from oven and let rest for 5 minutes.
  7. Plate and enjoy.

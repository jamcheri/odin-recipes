Chicken Thighs

Description

This recipe is simple but powerful. The ingredients are cheap, the steps can be followed by anyone, and the end result is naturally delicious.

Ingredients

  • Boneless Skinless Free-Range Chicken Thighs: 3 pounds
  • Glass Pan
  • Oven
  • Time: Approximately 60 minutes

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Add chicken thighs to pan
  3. Place pan in oven and cook for 45 minutes.
  4. Remove pan from oven and let rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Plate and enjoy.
